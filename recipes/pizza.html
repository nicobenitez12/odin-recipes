Pizza

This is the final result of a homemade pizza. If followed correctly you must be able to have a similar result

    List of ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
    Steps to follow:
  1. Mix flour, yeast and warm water in a bowl
  2. Amazar all the ingredients until we have a single dough
  3. Let the dough rest for 2 hours in the container until it doubles it size
  4. Extend the dough until you have a round shaped bread
  5. Add cheese and Pepperoni
  6. Bake it for 20 min until ready
