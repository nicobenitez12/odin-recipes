This is the final result of a homemade lasagna. If followed correctly you must be able to have a similar result

    List of ingredients:
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Cheese
  • meat
  • white sauce
  • Water
  • Tomato sauce
    Steps to follow:
  1. Boil the lasagna sheets while preparing the marinara with the meat and tomato paste
  2. In a baking tray put the lasagna sheets, then the marinara sauce and finally the white sauce
  3. Repeat step two until you are out of lasagna sheets while stacking the pasta and ingredients
  4. Add the cheese on top and grill it in the oven for min
